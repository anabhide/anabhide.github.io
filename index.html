About Me

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, advised by Prof. Peter J. Haas and Prof. David Jensen. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, causal inference, and simulation modeling. I am interested in using causal models to represent complex simulation systems and support causal reasoning and decision-making in settings where simulation is expensive.

I am also interested in probabilistic machine learning, particularly flow-based and diffusion-based generative models. Previously, I completed my bachelor’s degree at the University of Alberta, where I was a TA for CMPUT 304 and CMPUT 340 and worked as a Research Assistant in the RLAI lab under Prof. Rupam Mahmood. I have also interned at Synopsys Inc. as a Software Engineering Intern.

I am always happy to discuss research and possible collaborations. Feel free to reach me at abhide@umass.edu.
